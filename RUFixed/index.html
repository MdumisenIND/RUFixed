RUFixed

RUFixed


We Fix Your Issues.


RUFixed, is a web based application that allows Rhodes University computer repairs representatives to manage computer repair jobs as effectively and efficiently as possible. The application will also provide the Rhodes University customers the functionality and features to view various information pertaining to the device handed in for repairs as well as other services offered by RUFixed; which are managed by the respective and authorised parties within the department..

Our Values


MISSION: Our mission at RUFixed is to provide quick, reliable and customer-care driven device repair services.


VISION: Our vision is to be the best cloud-based computer repair system on the market. This will be achieved through efficient repair management, informative communication with customers and with our suppliers.
